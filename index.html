How to use this page
  • Click Show All to list all artists, albums, or songs.
  • To find a specific item, type its ID in the box and click Find (or press Enter).
  • Use Albums by Artist or Songs by Album to filter lists.
  • Tables are sortable — click a column header to sort. Click any row to open details.
  • If you need raw data for grading, enable Show JSON.

Use the buttons below to browse the music database. Results appear in readable tables — click a row to see details.
